Recipients of the travel awards have been selected and notified. We thank our sponsors for making it possible to support 13 participants from around the world.

We are committed to supporting researchers from diverse backgrounds and institutions to attend our workshop. Travel awards will be available to help cover registration and travel expenses for qualifying participants.

Priority will be given to students, postdocs, and researchers from underrepresented groups or institutions with limited funding.

How to Apply: Travel award applications are now integrated with paper submissions. When you submit your paper through OpenReview, you will see an optional section for requesting financial aid. Simply check the box "I would like to be considered for financial aid" and provide a brief justification explaining your need for financial support.

Questions: If you have any questions about the travel awards, please send an email to brainbodyfm@googlegroups.com.
